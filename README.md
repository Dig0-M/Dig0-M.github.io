## Final Project Description
__3D-City Simulation__

A 3D-City Sandbox that contains a randomized number of buildings in various positions and different styles. The placement of the buildings will always change every time!
The city is placed within a small enclosed environment that is wrapped around with a sky texture to give it some life and depth. As well as giving the ground a texture to make sure it all blends nicely together. You can also tweak the focal points of the 3D scene to your liking, as well as some secret lighting buttons I have hidden to play around with the lighting.
> Things you can do:
- You may walk around the city.
- Change the lighting sources of the city.
- Change the shading models to change the feel of the City.
- Decide what the weather will be like in the City.

A fun and interactive way to view 3D graphics using WebGPU and ray tracing via a bustling Cityscape!
